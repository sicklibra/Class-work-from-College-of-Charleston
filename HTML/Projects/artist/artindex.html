Who was he?

Maxfield Parrish lived from 1870-1966. The son of a painter,Stephen Parrish, art was in his blood. Being exposed to the beautiful architecture of the world, and the art of the old masters, his skill and popularity quickly grew to the master we know today.

Gallery

Maxfield Parrish has created nearly 900 independent works of art in his career. He was an illustrator for many childrens books as well as a comission artist, and muralist. His style became so renowned they even named a color after him "Parrish Blue". His works have been featured in museums and private collections globally.
