Welcome To The First Annual Plunder Party March 7 Spring Break 2023

At this First one of a kind event starting at 1900 (7:00pm) at the CofC Cistern, theTheater Department and History Department bring the scurvey side of the history of Charleston to Life! Blackbeard, Calico Jack, and Anne Bonnie are all expected to lay siege to the CofC cistern. They will be handing out "boons" to the champions of both physical and intelectual challanges. Aside from the great headliners that will serenade our pillaging party, you will be able to feast like a buccaneer with our various food and drink provided by the food truck caravan anchored on George st.

So, don your best pirate garb and partake with the crew!

The Event Rundown!

Music

The music for this event will be provided by some of the best fantasy rock bands as well as a guest appearance by the legendary infected mushroom for the late dance party.

  • Pregame with the scurvey crew of Alestorm. The drunken pirates themselves will be here to steal your beer as a start!
  • The powerful dwarves of the mining bards, Windrose will charge the warrior spirit of even the most timid cougar.
  • Last, we will dance the night away with the legendary Infected Mushroom. These EDM titans will have you dancing till you drop until 2:00am

The Booty!

The real treasure of this event is found here. Participants will be competing for tuition grants totaling $30,000 provided by our gracious supporters at Trident Construction.

Competitions

  • A nautical obstacle course that will include a snatch block heave,cargo net climb, and the capstan crank will be available. The top two placements in this event will recieve their piece of the grant. Liability Waivers must be completed prior to attendance, and medical staff will be on standby.
  • Put your math and reasoning to the test with a navigation drill through the spanish main. You will have to plot the course for the Queen Annes Revenge to lay siege to the island of jamaica. Once youve arrived, you must stretch your strategic skill to take down the fortifications and the island.
  • The last piece of the pie goes to the winner of a simple chess tournament. The first round will start at 6:00pm to take advantage of the calm before the storm. thechessstore.com is sponsoring this event and has sweetened the plunder with a custom pirate themed chess board for the champion. Participants must sign up by 8:00pm March 6th to allow time to create the brackets.
